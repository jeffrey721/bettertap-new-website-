Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on BetterTap systems within the continental United States. Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days, and most systems arrive within 5–7 business days of dispatch.

Because BetterTap is professionally installed, our team will coordinate with you to schedule an installation appointment once your system ships. Replacement filters and accessories are shipped directly to you and don't require an appointment.

You'll receive tracking information by email as soon as your order is on its way. If your address is outside our standard service area, please contact us before ordering so we can confirm availability.

Returns

We want you to love your water. If something isn't right, reach out to our team and we'll make it right.

Unused replacement filters and accessories in their original, unopened packaging may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund of the product price. For installed systems, contact us within 30 days and we'll walk you through the options available for your order.

To start a return, message us through Contact with your order number. Refunds are issued to your original payment method once the returned item is received and inspected.

Warranty

Every BetterTap system comes with a 3-year warranty, extendable to 5 years, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal residential use.

The warranty covers the system itself. Consumable parts that are replaced as part of normal upkeep — such as the MAZE filter and UV lamp — are not covered by the warranty, but you'll always know when they're due thanks to the system's built-in tracking.

To keep your coverage valid, use genuine BetterTap parts and follow the care steps in our guides. The official U.S. model is built for 110V and serviced here — grey-market imports are not covered. To make a claim, contact our team with your order details.

Payment Plans & Cancellation

BetterTap offers a few ways to pay — pay in full, installments, or lease — all described on the Shop page and confirmed at checkout. The clauses below explain how the recurring plans work and how to cancel.

How recurring plans work

If you choose an installment or lease plan, you authorize us to charge your payment method automatically on a regular schedule until the plan is complete or cancelled. We'll show you the payment amount, frequency and total before you commit, and we'll email a reminder if a payment can't be processed so you have a chance to update your details.

Your 30-day satisfaction window

We want you to love your water. You may cancel a new plan within 30 days of installation for any reason. We'll arrange to recover the system if it was leased, stop future charges, and refund what you've paid for the plan, less any non-refundable installation cost where applicable. Reach out through Contact with your order number to start.

Cancelling after the satisfaction window

After the first 30 days, installment plans run until the balance is paid in full. Lease plans continue for the minimum term shown at checkout and can be cancelled afterward with reasonable notice; on cancellation of a lease you agree to allow us to recover the system in good condition, fair wear and tear aside. We may pause or end a plan if scheduled payments fail repeatedly after we've tried to reach you, and we'll always tell you first.

What's included while your plan is active

Active lease plans include service and support to keep your system running — covering repair or replacement of the unit for faults that aren't caused by misuse. Routine consumables you replace as part of normal upkeep are handled separately and aren't part of the plan fee unless your checkout terms say otherwise.
