Why We Exist

We believe better water shouldn't be complicated — or wrapped in plastic.

For years, getting clean water meant choosing your compromise. Bottled water is expensive and piles up as plastic waste. Pitchers are slow, run warm, and need constant refilling. Reverse osmosis strips out the healthy minerals along with the bad stuff — leaving water that's clean but flat.

We thought the answer should be simpler: one connected system that removes what's harmful and keeps what's healthy. Powered by MAZE filtration, BetterTap delivers purified hot or cold water on demand — no bottles to haul, no pitcher to refill, no compromise to make.
