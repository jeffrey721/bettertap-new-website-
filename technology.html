What it removes

Five categories of filtration.

MAZE+UV works across every chemical, physical and biological category — each stage doing what the others can't. Tap a stage to see exactly what it targets.

The first stage clears the visible stuff — sediment, rust, dirt and debris that enter through aging pipes — so every stage that follows performs at full strength for the cartridge's whole life.

SedimentRustDirtDebris

A finer pass catches the tiny particles you can't see but that slip past the pre-filter. By this point the water runs physically clear, so the next stages focus entirely on chemical and biological filtration.
